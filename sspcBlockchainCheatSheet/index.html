The "Closing the Loop" Data Lifecycle

Hover each node for implementation details.

HSE / Government Issues secure, blockchain-verified prescription / batch

⚙️ How it works

Smart Contracts on an Ethereum Private PoA chain mint a unique prescription NFT per batch—tamper-proof and auditable by any authorised validator node.

Distribution / Pharmacy Tracks provenance; zero-counterfeit delivery

⚙️ How it works

Supply-chain oracles and GS1 EPCIS events write custody transfers to the ledger. Each scan updates the token's state, ensuring full provenance from factory to patient.

The Patient Adherence & usage data logged (privacy-preserved)

⚙️ How it works

Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) allow patients to attest adherence without revealing personal health data. A zk-SNARK proves "I took the medicine" without exposing identity.

Efficacy Data (RWE) Real-world evidence fed back into ledger

⚙️ How it works

On-chain analytics oracles aggregate anonymised outcome data into immutable efficacy scores. Federated learning models train across hospital nodes without centralising raw data.

HSE / Regulators Immutable efficacy reports inform policy

⚙️ How it works

Regulatory dashboards query the ledger in real-time via GraphQL APIs. Smart-contract–generated compliance certificates are automatically issued when efficacy thresholds are met.

Why Blockchain + Pharma?

  • €10.2 billion annual EU cost of counterfeit medicines [1]
  • Up to 50 % of patients are non-adherent to long-term medications, with much of this non-adherence remaining undetected without real-world evidence monitoring [2][3]
  • Zero-trust provenance removes intermediary audit lag [4]
  • GDPR-compliant via ZKPs and selective disclosure [5]

Why Now? The Window of Opportunity

EU Falsified Medicines Directive

The FMD mandates end-to-end serialisation — blockchain is the natural enforcement layer for immutable provenance.

Post-COVID Supply Chain Awareness

Pandemic-era drug shortages and quality incidents have created urgent institutional demand for transparent, resilient supply chains.

GDPR & Data Sovereignty

Growing regulatory scrutiny demands privacy-by-design architectures. ZKP-enabled blockchain is uniquely positioned to meet these requirements.

Blockchain Technology Maturity

Enterprise Ethereum and ZKP tooling have reached production readiness, making pilot deployments technically and economically viable now.

Proposed Research Questions

  1. 1 How can permissioned blockchain architectures guarantee end-to-end pharma supply-chain provenance while satisfying GDPR?
  2. 2 What is the optimal zero-knowledge proof scheme for privacy-preserved patient adherence reporting?
  3. 3 Can on-chain real-world evidence accelerate HSE drug-reimbursement decisions compared to traditional RCTs?
  4. 4 How can blockchain-enabled traceability and immutable real-world evidence improve patient outcomes through enhanced medication adherence monitoring and personalized treatment optimization?
